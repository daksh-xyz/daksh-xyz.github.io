The vision of Rotaract Club is to exchange ideas with leaders in the community,
develop leadership and professional skills, have fun through service and contribute for Nature.
        
About Rotaract

       The Rotaract Club MUJ has been an active social work club since many academic years, and has continued to aid a helping hand for the benefactor factor for the Society.
       Since its inception, the club has motivated the students or the Y-Generation to take initiative, exchange ideas with communities,
       develop professional and leardership skills and have fun through service. From social awareness, technical solutions, to more evident clean drives and plantation,
       the club has been working extensively on such projects and pioneers path breaking ideas for the future. All of this mostly rests in the hands of our upcoming youth,
       We here are transforming the youth by giving them a chance to do something for somebody in need and sense the self-fullfillment that comes in the process.
       
Objectives of the club

       The aim of the club is to promote environmental stability, prepare youth minds for powerful world, and create a greener tomorrow.
       We tend to promote overall sustainability, be it in terms of the environment or societal. 
       We at the Rotract Club have always worked towards the betterment of every aspect - health, environment, future sustainability - in the most fun and impactful ways.
